Beaches

Beach vacations are typically more laid back and relaxing since the water is already there for you to enjoy. There are plenty of water sports available at most beaches, such as swimming, snorkeling, surfing, or listening to music.

Mountains

Besides being a source of beauty and happiness, mountains prepare you for the fights of life. They not only challenge your physical stamina but mental strength too. Sometimes abstract can teach you more than living beings.
